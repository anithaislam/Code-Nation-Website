About Me

The Journey Begins

Hello. My name is Anitha Islam and I'd like to welcome you to my website.


My journey to coding via Code Nation has not been straight forward. I used to work in a multinational on various projects where I was testing the "front-end" of systems. I was the "user" checking that the system did what we wanted to do. I was always fascinated by the coding team who wrote the actual code to instruct the system to work. Jump ahead 10 years and two children later and I decided to dip my toe into the world of coding and here I am!!
